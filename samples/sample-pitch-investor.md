**Concept Pitch: Southern Roast Co. — growth capital for a second site and roastery expansion**
Prepared for: prospective investor

## Positioning
Southern Roast Co. is building a much-loved specialty coffee brand in the Southern Tablelands, known for exceptional coffee, warm hospitality and a strong connection to our local community. We believe there is huge potential to grow this into a beloved regional name.

## The opportunity
There is strong demand across the region for quality coffee, and we believe our brand resonates with a broad customer base of coffee lovers, professionals and families across the Southern Tablelands.

## Financial projection
Our existing café currently generates strong, consistent trade. We project the second site will perform similarly, reaching profitability within its first year. Combined, we forecast the two-site business generating approximately $850,000 in annual revenue, with a target gross margin of 35%.

## Why us
We've poured our heart into building this brand from day one, and our customers can feel that passion in every cup. We're committed to doing whatever it takes to make this business a success, and we believe our dedication is what will set us apart as we grow.

## The ask
We are seeking $150,000 in growth capital in exchange for a 15% equity stake, with funds to be deployed within the first three months toward fit-out, roasting equipment and working capital for the second site.
