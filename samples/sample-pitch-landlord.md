**Concept Pitch: Marlowe & Co.**
Prepared for: the leasing agent, re: vacant tenancy, Comur Street, Yass

## Positioning
Marlowe & Co. is a warm, welcoming café bringing quality coffee, fresh food and genuine community connection to the heart of Yass. We want to create a space where locals feel at home from their very first visit — a true community hub for Comur Street.

## Target customer
Our customers will be locals of all ages — families, young professionals, retirees, and visitors passing through Yass on the Hume Highway. We believe there's something for everyone at Marlowe & Co.

## Financial projection
We project average daily trade of 55 covers at an average transaction value of $13, giving weekly revenue of approximately $5,005. Food cost is budgeted at the industry-standard 30%, and we're targeting break-even within six months of opening.

## Why us
I have always been passionate about coffee and hospitality, and have dreamed of running my own café for years. I've spent the last two years researching the industry and visiting cafés around the country, and I know I have what it takes to make this a success.

## The ask
We're seeking a 5-year lease with an option to renew, and would appreciate the landlord's support with initial fit-out costs to help us open sooner.
